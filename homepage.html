Rotaract originally began as a Rotary International youth program in 1968 at Charlotte North Rotary Club in Charlotte,North Carolina, United States, and has grown into a major organization of over 10,904 clubs spread around the world and over 203,000 members in 189 countries.It is a service,leadership,professional and community service organization for young men and women aged 18 and over.
